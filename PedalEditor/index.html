PedalEditor

Deep MIDI Control for your hardware signal chain.

PedalEditor is a high-performance MIDI utility designed for musicians who need precision control over their hardware. By bridging your iOS device and your hardware via your local network, PedalEditor provides a seamless interface for sound design, preset management, and real-time parameter automation.

Core Features
Wireless Connectivity
Uses standard rtpMIDI to communicate over your local Wi-Fi network. No cables required.
Hardware Integration
Designed specifically to handle the CC and PC messages required by modern effects pedals.
Zero-Cloud Privacy
Your presets and MIDI data never leave your local network. No accounts, no tracking.
Optimized for iPad/iPhone
A responsive UI that puts the most important knobs exactly where you need them.
Technical Requirements

PedalEditor requires Local Network Permissions to discover and communicate with MIDI sessions on your Mac or hardware bridge. Ensure your device is on the same Wi-Fi network as your MIDI host.

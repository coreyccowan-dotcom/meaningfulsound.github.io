No Data Collected

Our apps do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no account systems, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs included in our software.

Device Permissions

Depending on the app, we may request specific permissions to enable core functionality:

  • Local Network: Used solely for MIDI communication between your devices. No data leaves your local network.
  • Microphone: If applicable, used only for audio analysis (such as tuning). No audio is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted.

No Third Parties

Meaningful Sound does not share information with third parties because no information is ever harvested or collected from your device.

Children

Our apps do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13, and are safe for all ages.
